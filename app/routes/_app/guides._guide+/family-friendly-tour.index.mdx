Yantian District (Yántián Qū, 盐田区) is a stunning destination where mountains meet the sea, offering a perfect family-friendly getaway. This 2-day tour is designed with families and children in mind, featuring the unique charm of **Zhongying Street (Zhōngyīng Jiē, 中英街)**, the breathtaking coastal views along **Meisha Road (Méishā Lù, 梅沙路)**, and the luxurious experience of **MGM Grand Hotel** and **OCT East (Huáqiáochéng Dōngbù, 华侨城东部)**. Enjoy scenic walks, shopping, beach fun, and five-star hospitality in one of Shenzhen's most picturesque areas.
